# CYBER CRIME CASE STUDY : SYNTHETIC IDENTITY BREACH
## INTRODUCTION
Identity theft is the unauthorized use of an individual’s personally identifiable information (PII)—such as Social Security numbers (SSNs), biometric data, or financial credentials—to commit fraud. This crime has evolved beyond simple credit card theft into Synthetic Identity Fraud, where criminals blend real data with AI-generated information to create "Frankenstein" identities that are nearly impossible for traditional systems to detect.
## HOW THE FRAUD HAPPENS (STEP-BY-STEP)
1. Criminals acquire PII through massive corporate data breaches or SMS phishing.
2. Using AI, the thief combines a real SSN(Social Security Number) with a fake name and address to create a synthetic identity.
3. They open a small credit card using this fake person. They pay the bills on time for a few months to make the person look like a responsible, real human.
4. Once the credit limit is high, the thief maxes out the card on expensive electronics or gift cards and disappears.
5. You only find out months later when a debt collector calls you about a person you've never heard of using your Social Security number.
## WHO IS USUALLT TARGETED?
* The Peak Earners (Ages 40–49)
* Seniors (80+)
* Minors
## THE CONSEQUENCES
* Financial Ruin
* Emotional Distress
* Legal & Administrative Burden
## KEY TAKEAWAYS
* Enable Multi-Factor Authentication
* Monitor Minors
* Do not use public Wi-Fi
