# STAY SAFE ONLINE CHECKLIST
## GENERAL SAFETY TIPS
1. Always verify calls/messages claiming to be from banks.
2. Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone. 
3. Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links.  
4. Use strong and unique passwords for accounts.

 ## UPI / ONLINE PAYMENT SAFETY
 1. Always check receiver details before sending money.
 2. Use only official apps (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc).
 3. Never approve unknown payment requests.
 4. Remember: Receiving money does NOT require a PIN.
